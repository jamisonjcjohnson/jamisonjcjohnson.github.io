Batocera makes it easy to change how your system looks without touching the command line. Themes control the layout, artwork, animations, and overall presentation of the interface.

In this blog post, we will walk through how to **download new themes**, **switch between them**, and **customize theme options** directly from the Batocera menu.

<br>

## Downloading New Themes

Batocera includes a built-in theme downloader.

To download themes:

- Press **Start** on your controller
- Go to **Updates & Downloads**
- Select **Themes**

You will see a list of available themes. To download one:

- Highlight the theme
- Select it
- The download will begin automatically

Once completed, the theme becomes available immediately.

<br>

## Changing the Active Theme

After downloading themes, you can switch between them at any time.

To change your theme:

- Press **Start**
- Go to **User Interface Settings**
- Locate the **Theme** option

You can:

- Press **Left** or **Right** on your controller to cycle through themes
- Or select the theme list and choose one directly

Once selected, press **B** to go back and the theme will apply.

<br>

## Theme Configuration Options

Many Batocera themes include additional configuration options.

To access them:

- Press **Start**
- Go to **User Interface Settings**
- Select **Theme Configuration**

Here you may find options such as:

- Layout styles
- Background behavior
- Artwork visibility
- Menu presentation

If you experiment with settings and do not like the result, you can always reset them to defaults.

<br>

## Additional Interface Settings

Beyond themes, Batocera offers other interface customization options:

- Screensaver behavior
- Showing or hiding folders
- Displaying specific menu items

These settings allow you to simplify the interface or tailor it to your preferences.
